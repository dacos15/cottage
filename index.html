Sunny Meadow

Approximately 200km from K-W area, this custom-built cottage sits on one acre of land and offers plenty of opportunities for recreational activities with its abundant wildlife in the area. The property is located just 1.7 kms from two water accesses to Lake Huron, making it an excellent spot for swimming, boating, paddle boarding, kayaking, watching the stunning sunsets, and more. Come home for a bbq on the wrap around covered porch while enjoying the view, peace, and privacy. While inside, enjoy a board game, cozy up by the wood stove, or relax to watch some movies, and, at night, enjoy the outdoor fire pit while gazing up at the stars.

Escape the busyness of the city and come relax at this spacious, private property. Bbq on the covered porch, lounge outside on the porch with your favourite book and beverage in hand or sit around the outdoor fire pit. The cottage is a warm, open concept main floor. One bedroom has a queen bed, while the other two have double beds, with comfortable mattresses.
